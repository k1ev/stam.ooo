1. Who we are

STAM is operated by 01001011 Stam BV, a company registered in The Netherlands (KvK 93086083). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you use our website (stam.ooo) and any applications or services we provide.

2. Data we collect

Website visitors: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics on stam.ooo. We do not collect personal data from website visitors.

App users: When you use one of our applications, we may collect data necessary for the app to function. The specific data collected depends on the app and is described in each app's individual privacy notice. We follow the principle of data minimization: we only collect what we need.

Email contacts: When you email us, we store your email address and message content to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing lists or share your email with third parties.

3. How we use your data

We use personal data solely to:

• Provide and improve our services and applications
• Respond to your inquiries
• Fulfill contractual obligations
• Comply with legal requirements

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. We do not use personal data for advertising or profiling.

4. Data storage and security

Data is stored on servers within the European Union. We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to personal data is limited to those who need it to provide our services.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. Email correspondence is retained for up to 2 years. App-specific data retention is described in each app's privacy notice.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

• Access the personal data we hold about you
• Request correction of inaccurate data
• Request deletion of your data
• Object to or restrict processing
• Data portability
• Lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens)

7. Third-party services

Some of our applications may use third-party services (such as cloud hosting, payment processing, or authentication providers). These services have their own privacy policies. We only work with providers that comply with GDPR and offer adequate data protection.

8. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. For significant changes, we will provide notice through our applications where applicable.
