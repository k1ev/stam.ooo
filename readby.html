readby
Record your voice reading stories. Play them back when you can't be there.
$ readby --describe
Parents travel. Parents work late. Parents aren't always there at bedtime.

ReadBy lets you record yourself reading physical books to your children — page by page, in your own voice. When you can't be there, they press play. Your voice fills the room. Story time happens anyway.
$ readby --features
feature voice recording — record audio per page of any physical book
feature page markers — tap to mark page turns while recording
feature book scanning — scan ISBN barcode to identify books automatically
feature family sharing — share recordings with family members
feature offline playback — recordings available without internet
feature sleep timer — auto-stop after the story ends
